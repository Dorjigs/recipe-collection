Quick Tips

  • Spice Culture

    Chili is considered a vegetable, not a spice in Bhutanese cuisine

  • Key Ingredient

    Cheese is a staple ingredient in most traditional dishes

  • Altitude Impact

    Mountain heights influence cooking methods and timing
